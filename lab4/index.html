Playing bass:

  1. Plug in your amp, but keep it off.
  2. Plug your jack into the input of the amp, connecting it to your bass.
  3. Turn the amp on, adjusting the volume, bass, treble, and mid dials accordingly.
  4. Play the bass, by strumming, plucking, or hitting your fingers along the four strings with your right hand, while your left hand is positioned along the frets to play a chord.
  5. Turn off the amp before removing the jack from your bass.
